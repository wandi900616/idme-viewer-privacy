Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 February 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how this application (“the App”) collects, uses, and protects user information.

1. Information Collection and Use

The App does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information on external servers.

However, the App may temporarily store the following data locally on your device only to enable app functionality:

  • Username
  • Password (stored securely using Android’s encrypted storage)

This data:

  • Is stored only on the user’s device
  • Is never transmitted to the developer or third parties
  • Is used only to automate login and navigation steps requested by the user

2. WebView Content

The App displays official web content using an embedded WebView.

All login actions and interactions occur directly between the user and the official website. The App does not intercept, modify, or record credentials entered on external websites beyond the automation explicitly requested by the user.

3. Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display rewarded advertisements.

AdMob may collect information such as:

  • Device information
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Usage data

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

The App does not control or store advertising data.

4. Automation Credits

The App includes an optional automation feature that uses a credit-based system.

  • Credits are stored locally on the device
  • Credits are used only to unlock automation features
  • Credits do not represent real-world monetary value

5. Data Security

The App uses Android’s EncryptedSharedPreferences to protect sensitive data stored locally.

Reasonable measures are taken to prevent unauthorized access, loss, or misuse of data on the device.

6. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services such as:

  • Google AdMob
  • Android System WebView

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

7. Children’s Privacy

This App is not intended for children under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact the developer via the app’s Play Store listing.
